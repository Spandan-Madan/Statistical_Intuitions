Short Bio

I am a Master's student at Harvard University and a research assistant at MIT co-advised by Fredo Durand, Aude Oliva and Hanspeter Pfister. My work focusses on computer vision and human perception studies for automatic understanding on multi-modal images. Before this, I completed my bachelors and master's from IIT Delhi. In the past, I was also a full time research engineer at UPenn.
